ABOUT ME

"Hi, my name is Ross Beckham. I am a Web Developer and Software Engineer. I have always had a passion with computers and took courses in college for Computer Science. Life took a turn so I had to take a break from school. I started working at a production plant as a production team leader that taught me quality, standardization, and team work skills that I now use in my software development career. I finally had the opportunity to attend TrueCoders Coding Bootcamp to continue my journey of learning software development. When I finished with TrueCoders, Crossvale gave me the chance to prove myself and helped me grow into who I am today.

I have two years of development experience with Crossvale Inc. helping multiple clients maintain code and build new features. As I went to each of my clients, I developed new skills with different technologies that allowed me to grow as a developer. In just a short time, I was exposed to full stack .NET applications and building out SQL databases to designing the front end of a newly built app using Angular and Typescript. I even had to learn the cloud to help one of my clients lift and shift their on premise system to Azure.

It has been a steep learning curve but has also been an exciting and fulfilling experience. I am currently learning full stack web development using Javascript, React, and Nodejs to broaden my knowledge at TrueCoders. With these skills I will create modern, responsive, quality websites and other applications. I would love the opportunity to have a conversation about my passion in the tech world with you!"
